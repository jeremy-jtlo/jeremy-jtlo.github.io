About Me

Who's This Guy?

My name's Jeremy! I'm a student of Computing Science at Simon Fraser University in Burnaby, British Columbia. I've finally completed my BSc at SFU, having focused mainly on full stack development, responsive design, and information design during my time here. As a challenge to myself (and an exercise in discipline), this site was made without the use of a front-end framework.

While my main interests are in the web and its workings, I do have prior experience as a test automation developer, leveraging tools such as Jenkins, vSphere, Citrix Xen, and more.

For hobbies, I thoroughly enjoy building Gundam models, sketching in my books or on a PC, and tinkering with my Linux installs.

Like what you see? Contact me on LinkedIn or send me an email, and I'll see what I can do.
